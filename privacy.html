Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Counter: Daily Habit Streak ("the App") as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or registration. Your habits, completion history, streaks, and preferences are stored only on your device and are never sent to any server.

Camera and Microphone

The App does not access your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, or location.

Analytics

The App uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to collect anonymous usage data such as which screens are viewed and general feature usage. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal information, device identifiers, or IP addresses. No data is shared with advertisers. For more information, see TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases, including a weekly subscription ($4.99/week with a 6-day free trial) and a lifetime access option ($24.99), processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. All purchase management, including refunds and cancellations, is handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Data Storage

All app data, including your habits, completion history, streak data, theme preferences, and purchase status, is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Notifications

The App may send local notifications as daily reminders if you enable them. Notification preferences are stored on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not contain age-restricted content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

RevenueCat — for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
TelemetryDeck — for anonymous, privacy-first analytics
Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
